Data Science Projects

(jun/2022) Sales forecast of a holding

Project made in Python to forecast sales for the next six weeks for each store of a drugstore holding company, using a database with different characteristics of each store. The project was carried out in several stages, from Exploratory Data Analysis to the interpretation of results. The forecast of the holding's sales within the stipulated period is $283,882,432. In the worst case, sales will be $283,142,716 and in the best, $284,622,126.

Tools used:

  • Python, Pandas, Numpy, Seaborn, Scikit-learn, Pickle, Boruta;
  • Jupyter Notebook;
  • Descriptive Statistics;
  • Rescaling, Transformation, Pearson's Correlation;
  • Linear Regression, Lasso, Random Forest, XGBoost;
  • Hyperparameter Fine Tune.

(dec/2021) Definition of buying and selling real estate

Insight study conducted in Python to identify the best buying and selling opportunities of properties of a fictitious company, using a database with several characteristics of the properties. Descriptive statistics were used to help define the purchase and sale price of properties. The expectation of profit would be between $96,220,810 and $244,216,541.
